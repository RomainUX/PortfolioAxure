Creating Personas for Company Strategy Purposes (2011)

Romain Ducerisier

Task Analysis

In order to renew the communication material of the company's agents and improve the deliveries management, a study was requested to better understand the needs of the employees in the field.

The purpose of this study was to explain to thetechnical teams the workers activities, user needs and requirements to complete the tasks.

I therefore opted for Personas that allow to think according to user needs and open the discussion around the expectations and activities of the users.

About the Project

The activity of agents and equipment suppliers do not have a lot of time to follow on the interface their tour and respond to messages.

They put the human relationship at the center of their tour and spend a lot of time trying to understand the needs of the customers.

The application that will be provided must adapt to the agents' habits while reminding them of the tour and allowing them to adapt to the customers' demands.

Likewise, managers must be able to follow the routes and help agents if incidents slow down a tour or if an appointment is cancelled.

It is therefore essential to have a good understanding of what each agent does and what difficulties he/she is currently experiencing in carrying out his/her tasks.

The challenge

User Observation & Interviews

Based on User Observation, Documents Analysis, Interviews and Workshops, we determined the activity (what are the tasks), the tasks requirements, Management Expectations, User Specificities and Needs.

Providing Personas for User centered Decision Making

Personas are commonly used tools in UX design. It's a representation of the real target audience data, collected in previous research phases such as user interview.

Personas are archetypical users whose goals and characteristics represent the needs of a larger group of users.
