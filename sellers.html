Sells Support Application for the Fashion Industry (2014)

Romain Ducerisier

User research

The application allows L'oréal sellers to define a business strategy based on customer specificities, expected sells and possible bargains.

As it is a personal support for each sellers, the application gathers, customer informations, a calendar that reminds customers appointments, previous orders, sells KPIs and a sells assistant.

Satisfaction Survey

About the Project

The applications had to be an intelligent and adaptive solution that matches with each seller strategy to ease business without forcing them to learn new methodology.

The application was a joint ventiure between Microsoft, SAP, L'Oréal and my Company to provide a mobile solution that would work offline as well as online.
