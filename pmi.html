About the Project

As this tool was at the heart of efforts to address the illicit tobacco trade, the tool had to be easy to use for both governments and end users to globally track and trace tobacco and prevent illicit sells.

This product targets both

- Governments & WHO (using this tool to track Tobacco production and illicit trade)

- Manufacturers to define and track the production volume of goods and the chain supply from factories to retailers.

Impacts of my work

Codentify has helped WHO to monitor and study the impact of counterfeit and illegally sold cigarettes on tobacco consumption around the world.

It emerged that the majority of cigarette packs that are sold illegally were manufactured in official factories.

This tool therefore made it possible to highlight the need for greater monitoring by gouvernments of the production and Trading of Tobacco.

Codentify, is a product serialisation/pack marker system, that allows to verify the authenticity of manufactured goods, whether they are genuine product or counterfeit.

 It details information about the supply chain process, including line and time of production, and adds a unique, random, set of 12 letters or numbers to each individual pack
