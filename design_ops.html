In order to define usable and efficient products, I defined a Quality based Design Ops to demonstrate my team's added value  and evangelize our Product Managers and Brand Owners to User Centered Design by demonstrating them the effciency of UX Design in our Agile Process and Product Design.


This Design Ops is mostly based on

- Standardized KPI for Satisfaction, Error Rate, Productivity and Task Completation

- Qualitative analysis of User Test's Results and friction points that are evaluated by degree of impact on tasks

- Close Collaboration with Sales Teams and Product Owners to Define, Enhance and Imagine the products that fit the most with our users needs.

About the Project

In a Company that makes top class Industrial Softwares for Native and Web environments since 1984, adding a new Design model requires the demonstration of its value for our custoimers and Dev teams that have to produce an high level of quality to remain the best in the industry and face growing competition.

The challenge

Kibana based Web Analytics that track UX KPI on specific events based Scenarios.

This allows the team and Product owners to check the impacts of proposals on taks completion in real time.

Our Solution

Each year starts with a global Heuristic Evaluation of our products based on :

- User tasks based scenarios

- PURE Method

- Bastien & Scapin Usability Criteria

- Solution proposals for each friction point

- Enhancement Request submission to my Team to define our focus of interest for the next weeks.

At Mid Year, we assess globally with final users our apps in User tests to challenge our proposals and new products and receive feedbacks and enhancements ideas from the most valuable source we can have : our consumers.

On side of these important mile stones, the team shares with product owners an Agile Design Management schedule that allows us to

- define and plan the subjects we will address in the coming year,

- decide the enhancements that will be delivered by dev teams

- track the amount of Enhancements treated by Dev Teams,

- Run workshops &  release goals with the team and key stakeholders.

Impacts of my work

This communication strategy facilitated understanding of the positive effects of Design on product's overall quality and improved Teams' understanding of "UX Design" and its benefits on each steps of the iterative development process.


With both quantitative and qualitative KPIs, the UX team is better integrated into the software design and improvement process.


Project Managers and Product designers also call on us more because they can see year after year the impacts of UX choices and the recurrence of usability problems that impact their software.
